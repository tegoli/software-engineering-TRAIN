PERFORMANCE METRICS
===============================================

Train search response time: 2 seconds
Initial page load: 4 seconds
Subsequent page navigation: 3 seconds
Real-time data refresh: Every minute or faster
Maximum concurrent users: 100,000
System uptime: 99% (maximum 7.2 hours downtime per month)
Session persistence: 24 hours of activity


SCALABILITY TARGETS
===============================================

Registered users: 500,000 minimum, scaling to 2 million over 5 years
Concurrent users: 100,000 without performance degradation
Data storage: Growing volume support from booking history and analytics
Traffic peaks: Automatic load balancing during rush hours and holidays


SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
===============================================

Password minimum length: 8 characters
Password complexity: 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 digit, 1 special character
Data transmission: TLS 1.2 or higher
Data at rest encryption: AES-256
Payment compliance: PCI DSS standards
Session timeout: 24 hours of inactivity


BROWSER AND DEVICE SUPPORT
===============================================

Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (from 2022 onwards)
Mobile support: Full responsive design for smartphones and tablets
Desktop support: Full support for desktop computers


COMPLIANCE AND REGULATIONS
===============================================

GDPR 2016/679 compliance required
Articles 17, 19, 32 specifically required
Data retention: 5 years for archived bookings, then automatic deletion
Privacy policy required and must be accepted during registration
Audit logging required for all administrative actions
Email confirmations for sensitive operations


ADDITIONAL FEATURES AND CONSTRAINTS
===============================================

Bike wagon specifications:
- Reserved for non-folding bikes only
- Limited to approximately 7 spaces per wagon
- Subject to additional cost

Ticket modification:
- Extended changes allowed for delays > 15 minutes
- Standard modifications have limitations

Train delay prediction:
- Integration with external ML service
- Provides probability for each train option

Analytics and reporting:
- Revenue per route and per train run
- Reservation count monitoring
- Most frequently used route identification
- Employee activity tracking

Data updates:
- Departure/arrival boards: Real-time (every minute)
- Occupancy tracking: Real-time (every minute)
- Capacity insights: Real-time (every minute)

Accessibility:
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance required
- Multiple language support: English, Italian, German, French
- Appropriate localization for dates, currencies, formats
